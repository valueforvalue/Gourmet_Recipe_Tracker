RECIPE: Gourmet Cheeseburger Soup
TAGS: soup

INGREDIENTS
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter
- 1 cup carrots, diced
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/2 cup crispy bacon bits
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup dill pickles, diced
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

INSTRUCTIONS
1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat and sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened.
2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, draining excess fat if necessary.
3. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it forms a paste.
4. Gradually add the chicken broth while stirring to avoid lumps, then bring to a simmer.
5. Add the heavy cream, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and smoked paprika, and let simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Reduce the heat to low and slowly add the cheddar cheese, stirring until melted and smooth.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then stir in fresh parsley and crispy bacon bits.
8. Serve hot topped with sour cream, diced pickles, and halved cherry tomatoes.
