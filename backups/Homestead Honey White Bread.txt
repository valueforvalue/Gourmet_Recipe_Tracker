RECIPE: Homestead Honey White Bread
TAGS: Bread

INGREDIENTS
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 packet active dry yeast (2 1/4 tsp)
- 2 cups warm water (110°F)
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
- 2 tsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS
1. In a large bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes.
2. Stir in honey, melted butter, and salt into the yeast mixture.
3. Gradually add flour, one cup at a time, until a dough forms.
4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
6. Punch down the dough, divide it in half, and shape each half into a loaf.
7. Place loaves in greased 9x5 inch loaf pans, cover, and let rise again until doubled, about 30 minutes.
8. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
9. Bake loaves for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and sound hollow when tapped.
10. Let cool on a wire rack before slicing.
