RECIPE: Zesty Tuna Quinoa Patties
TAGS: 

INGREDIENTS
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS
1. In a large bowl, combine drained tuna, cooked quinoa, egg, breadcrumbs, parsley, onion, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
2. Shape the mixture into 8 small patties using your hands, pressing them firmly to ensure they hold together.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Place patties in the skillet and cook for about 4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
5. Remove from skillet and transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to cool slightly before serving.
