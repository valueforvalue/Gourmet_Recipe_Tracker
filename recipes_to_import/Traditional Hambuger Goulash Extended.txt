RECIPE: Traditional Hamburger Goulash Extended
TAGS: Soup Hamburger

--- INGREDIENTS ---
1 lb ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups beef broth
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 cup elbow macaroni
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp olive oil
--- INSTRUCTIONS ---
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until the onion is translucent.
3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
4. Stir in tomato sauce, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
5. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then add elbow macaroni.
6. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
